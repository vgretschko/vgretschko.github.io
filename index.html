About

Profile

I am an economist specializing in empirical methods and policy evaluation, with a focus on labor markets, public finance, and development economics. My research combines administrative data, field experiments, and structural modeling to evaluate how policies affect households and firms.

My scholarship has been published in leading journals and informs policymakers, NGOs, and research institutions. I regularly teach graduate and undergraduate courses in econometrics and public economics.
